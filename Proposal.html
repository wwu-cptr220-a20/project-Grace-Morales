Art Stage

Topic Overview

For my project I propose a website created to advertise and sell my art pieces and photography. In addition I would like the website to also include a space to share explanations of my art and photography process, and tips for amateur artists and photographers. So this website would educational for beginners, as well as showcase the work that results from what I would be discussing with said beginners.

I want my website to be structured this way so that I can simultaneously sell the work I'm proud of and help others who want to learn. It's a good way for customers to fully appreciate the product they're buying. It also provides the opportunity for engagement for those that want to try to develop new artistic skill sets.

It's difficult to find easily accessbile sources that discuss common beginner mistakes in art and photography and useful tips to fix those mistakes. There are plenty people that teach courses and classes that require payment and registration, but my website will be accessible and also provde results for what I discuss.

Potential Applications

Art Transfer App
This app would be particularly useful for amateurs who would want to transition to digital art whether as a main medium or to better edit their art. This would be an app that could use a scanning device or just take a picture of the art and turn it into a digital file. An app that can make these listed steps more efficient.
Photo Perspective App
This app will help users understand perspective. Simply take a picture of your subject, whether it's just a landscape or of a person. The app will then show several picture options of optimal perspectives the picture can be edited to look like. Similar to the SKRWT App
Lighting Editing App
Lighting is a big part of photography, and frankly my favorite thing to play with besides perspective. Any budding photographer will know how essential lighting and editing lighting can be for photos. This app would be primarily for fixing lighting and changing the lighting for the most optimal photos to really set their mood. Similar to what I primarily use for lighting: Adobe Lightroom Photo Editor App
